RECRUITING THE TALENT

  • We are not body brokers.
  • We don’t make it rain resumes.
  • We don’t have daily call requirements – you’ll hear from us when we have talent or a cool story to tell about one of our students.
  • We invest 100% of our profits back into the talent pipeline in Colorado – in 5 years we’ll have more talent for you.
  • We may reach out to 100 people, interview the best 10 and will present the best one that fits your culture and your requirements. We have a team of tenacious recruiters who are professional and working for a purpose that is much greater than ourselves!
In our recruiting business, hiring managers partner with SeedPaths because we:
  1. Help them find great software engineers today.
  2. Invest 100% of our profit back into building out the technical talent pipeline.
  3. Provide paths for company employees to volunteer.
  4. Recognize them and their companies in the community for being awesome!

WHY US

Simply put, we help CTOs and hiring managers find great technologists today (variety of roles). This creates a long-term sustainable strategy where each business supports the other.

If we’re honored to work with your company, we’ll facilitate volunteering opportunities for you and your employees to volunteer through internships, mentorship and facilitating field trips.
