WHAT’s the scoop on cost?

The cost is $6,000 for our 8-week boot camp.

Full-ride scholarships are available for those who qualify based on need such as low income or other barriers to employment (youth offender, foster care, single parent no older than 21, disability, etc.). Funding for these scholarships is provided by local workforce development centers. For those who qualify, the program will have no cost to the participant. Please contact us if you’d like to see your level of eligibility.

For non-scholarship participants who do not qualify for federal funding, a payment of $3,000 is due upon being accepted to the program (and before the first day) and the other $3,000 due by the Friday of Week 4.

All participants will have opportunities to earn back $500 in incentives through performance metrics.

Deferred payment plans exist on a case-by-case basis for those who can’t make a full investment at this time. Our payment terms are 10% of your monthly gross salary after graduation or $100 per month (whichever is more, paid via direct deposit) plus a $20/month administration fee.

  • How old do I have to be to apply?

    Age is just a number! We love having diverse, wide-ranging age groups in our boot camps. The variety brings different per- spectives and viewpoints that will only help. We encourage anyone 16-30 years old to apply.
    What is the application process?

    Step 1: Apply through this website.
    Step 2: Attend an info session at SeedPaths on Tuesdays at 10:00 am or Thursdays at 1:00 pm.
    Step 3: Collect all necessary documentation and paperwork to qualify for funding assistance (if eligible).
    Step 4: Complete contract & pre-work assignments.
    Step 5: Accept your invitation into the program and began your journey into the IT industry!

  • Do I need my own computer?

    Nope. SeedPaths will provide you with all the supplies and materials you need to go through our boot camp.

  • Do I need to know anything about HTML or how to build websites?

    No, any experience you have will be helpful. To be successful, all you need is an open mind, the ability to pay attention and desire to get a job in the tech industry. The tech training is geared towards those with an interest in building web applica- tions, but may not have been exposed to the toolset yet.

  • What kinds of jobs will this program prepare me for?

    There are four sectors of the tech industry in which we will prepare our technologists:

    Software & Web Development
    Quality Assurance & Testing
    Sales and Marketing
    Customer Service

  • Will SeedPaths help me find a job?

    We will do everything we can to help you get employed within 90 days of completing the program, but there are no guarantees. During the professional development part of the program, we will teach you all the steps necessary to help you learn HOW & WHERE to find a job and the skills needed to earn that job. You’ll learn so much about job searches, you probably won’t even need us to help you. You’ll be that prepared.

  • Do participants receive college credit?

    At this time, no. All of the skills and experiences gained in this program as designed to prepare participants for internship or entry-level employment opportunities in the technology industry.

  • When will boot camps run in 2014?

    We plan to run five in 2014. The approximate dates for the others are:

    January 27 to March 21 – COMPLETED!
    April 7 to May 30 – IN PROGRESS!
    June 16 to August 8
    August 25 to October 17
    November 3 to January 9 (off Nov. 24-28 and Dec. 22-Jan. 2)
