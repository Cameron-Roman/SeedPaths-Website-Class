OUR GOAL

10 years. 10 cities. 10,000 new technologists every year.

We invest in young adults through experiential, hands-on, project-based learning.

We teach them technical skills to be strong developers and professional skills to be successful in life.

Through our work, a little life coaching and some job placement support, SeedPaths students go on to become successful individuals in their chosen paths.
